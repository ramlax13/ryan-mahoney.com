Vatican Internship



In the Spring of 2010 I Interned at the Office of the Internet at the Vatican in Rome. I spent 4 months abroad working on multimedia projects for the Vatican, including virtual reality tours for the Vatican website (click here). I kept a blog, updated daily with writing and photographs from my experiences and travels, which can be found by clicking 'blog' on the right, and took hundreds of photos, some of which can be found in the 'Photography' section of the website.
