Recent Project:

Night Traffic

I recently did a night shoot around Villanova's campus, mainly of the traffic that night. I set myself up on Spring Mill Road, on the Septa Train Station overpass, and experimented with exposure settings as cars whizzed by. As it got darker the lights off the cars and the sky became much more drastic, and just as I seemed to find the perfect combination of natural light and exposure, I snapped this picture.
